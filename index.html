The M-SIPA Platform

Five Layers of Financial Infrastructure

Layer 1

Community Network

Communities, NGOs, cooperatives, farmer groups, women’s associations, faith-based organisations and humanitarian partners.

Layer 2

Field Intelligence Layer

Local Agents use Mobile Devices for Offline Data Capture through Digital Forms, with support for Voice, USSD, Biometric Support, and GPS.

Layer 3

Behavioural Identity Engine

This becomes your intellectual property—capturing Savings, Attendance, Trading Activity, Repayment, Participation, Reliability, Community Trust, and Economic Behaviour. Instead of static identities, M-SIPA builds living economic identities.

Layer 4

Financial Enablement Layer

M-SIPA brings together Banks, MFIs, Fintechs, Wallets, Insurance providers, Government Transfers, Agricultural Finance, Merchant Payments, Savings, and Credit into a unified ecosystem.

Layer 5

Intelligence & Analytics Layer

AI powers a competitive moat through Risk Scoring, Portfolio Analytics, Community Health, Agricultural Productivity, Livelihood Indicators, Programme Monitoring, Economic Insights, Development Impact, and Policy Intelligence.
